Company Overview

Easy-Ark Invest is a Hong Kong-based asset management firm licensed by the Securities and Futures Commission for Type 4 (Advising on Securities) and Type 9 (Asset Management) regulated activities. We focus exclusively on Professional Investors as defined under the Securities and Futures Ordinance.

Our firm operates at the intersection of institutional fund management, systematic alpha generation, and proprietary research. Through our Limited Partnership Fund structures, we deliver flexible, tax-efficient investment vehicles to sophisticated investors seeking differentiated exposure and disciplined portfolio management.

Our licensed representatives actively contribute to leading alpha capture platforms globally, participate in institutional investment programmes and competitions, and publish proprietary research across equities, derivatives, and alternative strategies.

Our Approach

We employ a multi-strategy approach combining fundamental research, quantitative analysis, and systematic idea generation. We believe in disciplined portfolio construction, rigorous risk management, and transparent reporting.

Every investment decision is underpinned by a clearly defined process: proprietary research identifies opportunities, quantitative models assess risk-reward characteristics, and portfolio construction ensures diversification within defined risk budgets. This systematic discipline — applied consistently across market cycles — is the foundation of our alpha generation capability.

Fund Structures

We operate through Hong Kong Limited Partnership Fund (LPF) structures — a modern, flexible, and internationally recognised vehicle for professional fund management. The LPF regime, established under Hong Kong law, provides an onshore fund structuring option that meets institutional standards.

Tax Pass-Through Treatment LPF structures benefit from tax transparency, with income and gains passing directly to limited partners
GP/LP Governance Clear separation of responsibilities between the General Partner (Easy-Ark Invest) and Limited Partners (investors)
Flexible Investment Mandates LPF structures accommodate a wide range of strategies across equities, fixed income, derivatives, and alternatives
Institutional-Grade Administration Independent fund administration, audit, and custodian arrangements ensuring operational integrity
