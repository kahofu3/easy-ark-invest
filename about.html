About Us

Our Firm & Approach

An SFC-licensed asset management platform built for Professional Investors, combining alpha generation, LPF fund structures, and proprietary research.

Company Overview

Easy-Ark Invest is a Hong Kong-based asset management firm licensed by the Securities and Futures Commission for Type 4 (Advising on Securities) and Type 9 (Asset Management) regulated activities. We focus exclusively on Professional Investors as defined under the Securities and Futures Ordinance.

Our firm operates at the intersection of institutional fund management, systematic alpha generation, and proprietary research. Through our Limited Partnership Fund structures, we deliver flexible, tax-efficient investment vehicles to sophisticated investors.

Our Approach

We employ a multi-strategy approach combining fundamental research, quantitative analysis, and systematic idea generation. We believe in disciplined portfolio construction, rigorous risk management, and transparent reporting.

Easy-Ark Invest is licensed by the Securities and Futures Commission of Hong Kong (Type 4 & Type 9). This website is for informational purposes only. Services are available exclusively to Professional Investors as defined under the Securities and Futures Ordinance.
